Resources for startups in Paris

2017-10-05
paris

Some resource to the french ecosystem of startups in Paris:

Friendly startups

  • Lulu dans ma rue has support from Mairie de Paris and provide door to door services for parisians.
  • Frichti is a fresh food delivery service. Has an original concept
  • Jam is a online assistant for young parisians to help them go organizing their student life.
  • Pretty Simple is an online game editor.

Community

  • Rude baguette is the best blog for news about the French startups scene.
  • L’Usine Digitale online news about french technology.
  • Meetup has a lot of tech events every week hosted in large companies parisian offices (Mozilla, Criteo, Le Bon Coin, Microsoft, Google, Societe Generale…)
  • Let’s Run Paris Cool Meetup group with expats (mainly Americans but not only) if you like running and want to discover the best routes in Paris.

Incubators

  • Station F is an incubator campus for startups. It is supported by Facebook and successful entrepreneur Xavier Niel.

Life in Paris

  • Teleport gives fair review for foreigners who might want to join Paris for IT jobs.
